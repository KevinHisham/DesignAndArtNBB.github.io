About Us

Design and Art (DnA) adalah salah satu divisi dalam unit kegiatan mahasiswa Nippon Bunka-bu yang bergerak dalam bidang seni visual, terutama ilustrasi dan animasi. Kegiatan Design and Art mencakup dari kumpulan bersama dimana kami mempelajari pembuatan ilustrasi serta animasi sambil bonding, hingga pembuatan animatic storyboard.

Design and Art (DnA) is a division from Telkom University's student activity unit Nippon Bunka-Bu that focuses on visual arts, especially illustration and animation. Design and Art activities range from gatherings where we learn about illustration and animation making while bonding together, to making animatic storyboards.
